What are Passkeys?

  • More Secure: Passkeys use public-key cryptography, making them resistant to phishing and data breaches.
  • More Convenient: No passwords to remember or type. Use your device's biometric authentication.
  • Cross-Platform: Works across different devices and platforms seamlessly.
  • Privacy-Focused: Your biometric data never leaves your device.
